Max Planck

The International Max Planck Research School on Astrophysics (IMPRS) in Garching/Munich (Bavaria, Germany) is soliciting applications for its PhD program. Please find attached more detailed information on the graduate school and its program. We would appreciate if you could distribute the information among interested students in the program.

Deadline for applications is November 1, 2022.

In addition to this announcement we have an IMPRS-poster available which we would ask you to print and to display for your students. The poster can be downloaded from our website:

https://www.imprs-astro.mpg.de/sites/default/files/imprs_poster_2023.pdf

If you do not want to receive this information any more please send
a short note to <office @ imprs-astro.mpg.de>.

Many thanks for your support and cooperation,

With best regards,

Prof. Dr. Werner Becker
